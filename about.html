Hello, my name is Joseph Pogoretskiy. I was born in a family of 7 in a small town of Verona, in Virginia. I started putting together loops in Garageband when I was around 9 years old, and was fascinated in it. I have created a few fun tunes and didn't lay a finger on the program for a 3 years. I started making music again once I got my hands on a DAW called FL Studio. I have created lots of tunes since then, and my quality has progressed over the months.

I love sushi, dogs, and music that pushes the boundaries of the human ear. I have recently started to learn web development and design, so here I am now. If you have any questions, or requests, please email me - josephpogoretskiy@gmail.com
